Say “hello” to Rake and Spade Landscape Gardners, where passion meets expertise in Halifax’s South-End.


With a partnership boasting more than 20 years of combined experience, our commitment to professionalism is unwavering. We take pride in our approach, eschewing heavy equipment in favor of handcrafted labor, ensuring precision and care in every project we undertake. Our designs marry high impact with low maintenance, creating outdoor spaces that captivate while requiring minimal upkeep. Serving the beautiful gardens of peninsular Halifax, concentrated in the elegant South End neighbourhood, we're dedicated to bringing beauty and sustainability to your home.
