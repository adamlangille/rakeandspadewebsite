About Us


Rake & Spade came about from an unlikely union.
Fate brought them together under the leadership of one of the longest running and most respected private gardeners operating in the HRM.

Rob Brown followed a winding career path that involved him owning and operating a successful landscaping company in New Brunswick for a number of years, then later pivoted into becoming a teacher. However, once the pandemic hit and schools were closed, Rob rekindled his love of gardening and as time moved forward he realized where his heart lies and hasn't looked back.

Jesse Jessome returned to Nova Scotia in 2018 after a 10 year career as a provincial property manager in Victoria BC. He had always enjoyed gardening on the side and felt it was time to pursue that passion more directly. He spent four years learning the trade from the best teacher available.
